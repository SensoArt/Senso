About

SENSO was born in 2025 from an idea by Claudia Alizieri and Adrian Villanueva, a vision shaped by intuition, passion, and the desire to create something meaningful.

At the beginning of 2026, this vision starts to take form, preparing to reveal itself through its inaugural event, Principium: In the beginning there was chaos.

Not simply an opening, but a moment of origin. A threshold. A quiet anticipation before creation.

SENSO emerges from the belief that art is not only to be seen, but to be felt. That every encounter with art can awaken perception, stir emotion, and guide direction. It is a space imagined as a journey, where senses become language and experiences leave a lasting trace.

Rooted in Barcelona and open to the world, SENSO grows from connection: between artists and audience, between vision and expression.

Because every creation begins in silence.
Every vision begins with a feeling.
And every beginning carries the promise of what is yet to come.
