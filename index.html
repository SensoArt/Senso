SENSO
Where Art becomes experience

ABOUT

SENSO is an artistic project that transforms art into a journey.

It captures in a single word: perception, emotion, direction.

A place where senses become language, emotions become experience, and direction becomes vision.

MISSION

To create an authentic space in Barcelona where art is not only seen, but felt and lived.
A place where emerging and international artists connect with the public through curated experiences.

VISION

To become an international reference in contemporary art, positioning Barcelona as a key stage for discovery, innovation, and unique artistic experiences.

FOUNDERS

Claudia brings artistic sensitivity and experience in gallery management and cultural curation.

Adri brings strategic vision and the ability to build meaningful cultural connections.

Together they shape SENSO: a space where art is lived, felt, and shared.
