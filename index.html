Principium

In the Beginning there was Chaos

where art becomes experience

EN — Before form, before order, before beauty, there was chaos. Principium emerges from the idea of exploring the origin of everything. The exhibition brings together contemporary artists creating an unstable balance of tensions.

ES — Antes de la forma, antes del orden, antes de la belleza, existía el caos. Principium nace de la idea de explorar el origen de todas las cosas. La exposición reúne a artistas contemporáneos generando un equilibrio inestable.
